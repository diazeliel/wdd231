Financial Education Website

Site Plan Document

Site Name and Rationale

Site Name: Financial Education

Rationale: This project encourages people by offering advice, practical courses, and resources. The platform will cover essential personal finance topics, including budgeting, saving, investing, debt management, and wealth building.

Site Purpose

The primary purpose of this website is to democratize financial knowledge and empower individuals to take control of their economic futures. Specifically, it aims to:

  • Establish and Promote Best Practices: Provide clear guidance on how to create, monitor, and adhere to a practical personal budget as a cornerstone of sound financial health.
  • Encourage Self-Reliance: Inspire and equip users to become more financially autonomous by cultivating the essential skills and knowledge necessary for effective personal financial management.
  • Drive Positive Behavior Change: Translate complex financial concepts into accessible, engaging, and easy-to-follow steps, facilitating immediate and positive financial action.

The ultimate goal is to help users develop the discipline and expertise needed for a sustained better financial practice.

Target Audience Scenarios

Where do I even start to get control of my finances?

All people, at every stage of life, need to know how to manage their money through a budget. If we learn to follow a budget from a young age, we can develop better financial habits and enjoy a higher quality of life. Proper money management helps us save for the future, avoid debt, and achieve our financial goals. Starting early and staying consistent with a budget is key to building a secure and comfortable lifestyle.

As an employee, what are my best options for earning more money?

Most people tend to be consumers, often spending money on things they don't need. Many of us lack knowledge about how to invest wisely, which can limit our financial growth and stability. Developing a better understanding of smart financial habits and investment strategies is essential for building a secure and prosperous future.

Color Schema

The following colors will be used throughout the website to create a professional, trustworthy, and engaging visual experience:

Primary Color
#2c3e50
Secondary Color
#3498db
Accent Color
#e74c3c
Background Color
#f9f9f9

Color Usage Guidelines

  • Primary Color (#2c3e50): Used for headings, important text, footer, and main navigation elements to establish trust and professionalism.
  • Secondary Color (#3498db): Used for links, buttons, highlights, and interactive elements to create engagement and guide user actions.
  • Accent Color (#e74c3c): Used sparingly for call-to-action buttons, important alerts, and special offers to draw attention to critical elements.
  • Background Color (#f9f9f9): Used as the main background color to create a clean, readable interface with sufficient contrast.

Typography

Primary Font: Source Sans Pro

Secondary Font: Roboto

Font Application

  • Source Sans Pro: Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3) and body text to ensure readability and professional appearance.
  • Roboto: Used for captions, quotes, and secondary information to provide visual hierarchy and variety.
