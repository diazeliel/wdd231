Find a Better Job

Discover practical guidance to find meaningful employment

Key Principles

  • Identify your unique talents and abilities
  • Develop marketable skills for today's economy
  • Create an effective resume and cover letter
  • Prepare for interviews with confidence
  • Network effectively and build relationships
  • Research companies and opportunities
  • Follow up professionally and persist

Reflection Questions

What unique talents and abilities do you possess that could be valuable in the workplace?
What skills do you need to develop to advance your career?
What specific steps will you take this week to improve your employment situation?

Personal Finances

Learn to manage your resources effectively

Key Principles

  • Live within your means and avoid debt
  • Build an emergency fund for security
  • Teach family members financial principles
  • Use a budget and track expenses
  • Save for long-term goals and retirement
  • Invest wisely for future growth
  • Be a responsible steward of your resources

Reflection Questions

How can you better manage your financial resources?
What changes do you need to make to become financially secure?
What financial goals will you set for yourself and your family?

Education for Better Work

Pursue education to increase your opportunities

Key Principles

  • Commit to lifelong learning and growth
  • Identify educational opportunities that align with your goals
  • Balance education with work and family responsibilities
  • Seek financial aid and scholarships when needed
  • Develop skills that are in demand in the job market
  • Apply what you learn to real-world situations
  • Use your education to create positive impact

Reflection Questions
